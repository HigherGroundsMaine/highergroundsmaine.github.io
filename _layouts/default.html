Blending the art of coffee with the rich tradition of craft cannabis
Locally Sourced

All of our products are sourced right here at home in the great state of Maine, where we work with a network of small crafters and artisans to deliver a truly unique experience.

We're proud to bring you Maine's locally-grown cannabis products, paired with a top-shelf specialty coffee menu. We also offer organically-grown hemp CBD products.

World-Class Products

We proudly serve wood-roasted organic Speckled Ax coffee alongside offerings from our world-class cannabis farmers. You can even have cannabis infused into your coffee!
